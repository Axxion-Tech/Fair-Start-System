What's really stopping pilots from jumping the tone?

Whether it's on purpose or unintentional, false starts are common in drone races. And if it's not too egregious, the race continues - since it would take too much work and time recalling everyone to the start blocks and changing out batteries.

Enter the Fair Start System!

Two servos control a front panel in the starting box, making it impossible to false start during a heat. Synchronized LED lighting and countdown tones give pilots an indication of when to be prepared to fly. And, completely wireless communication over bluetooth allows the starting box to be placed anywhere on the track.
